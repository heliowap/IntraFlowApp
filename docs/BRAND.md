# Intra Flow Brand Direction

Storytelling companion: [BRAND_STORY.md](BRAND_STORY.md).

## Core Idea

Impulse inside flow.

The brand is built around a living `I`: a text cursor that becomes the internal ordering
axis of the product. It sits where the keyboard used to dominate, but it receives voice.
Human speech, the primary form of communication, enters the digital writing surface and
becomes structured language.

## Relationship To Intrador

Intra Flow should carry Intrador DNA while remaining independent.

Inherited ideas:

- internal flow
- membrane-like passage
- bioelectric impulse
- fingerprint-like fluidity
- transformation from signal to useful action

Avoid:

- copying the Intrador logo
- looking like a clinic logo
- leaning on generic medical blues
- using biological references so literally that the app stops feeling like a macOS product

## Naming Logic

`Intra` means inside, inward, internal order.

`Flow` means movement, speech, propagation, language in motion.

The mark should express `I + flow`: the `I` is inside the flow and gives it orientation.
Without the `I`, flow is dispersion. With the `I`, flow becomes creation.

## Canonical Motion

The primary brand expression is kinetic-first:

1. The `I` appears as a text cursor.
2. The cursor pulses with a bioelectric rhythm.
3. Internal flow lines enter and begin misaligned.
4. The cursor orders the flow.
5. The word `flow` is born.

The static logo is a frame of this motion, not the original source of the identity.

## Visual Metaphor

Primary metaphor: a technical, systemic fingerprint or internal flow map.

Secondary metaphors:

- action-potential impulse
- membrane/channel passage
- insertion cursor
- ordered language forming from speech

Do not lead with a microphone, generic audio wave, chat bubble, or AI sparkle.

## Tone

Precision premium.

The brand should feel controlled, native to macOS, and carefully engineered. It should not
feel loud, clinical, magical, or decorative. The emotional register is confident and
quietly alive.

## Future Fork Constraint

A future medical-scribe fork should be able to inherit the system by shifting the accent
and content context, not by replacing the core identity. The living cursor and ordered
internal flow remain stable. Clinical calm can become a variant, not the parent identity.

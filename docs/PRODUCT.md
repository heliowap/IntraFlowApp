# Intra Flow Product Context

## Register

Product-first. Intra Flow is a working macOS tool, so app surfaces should feel native,
precise, and task-focused. Brand surfaces may use a stronger identity register, but they
must still resolve back into a trustworthy product UI.

## Product Purpose

Intra Flow is a native macOS voice-dictation app. The user triggers a Dictation, speaks,
and receives polished text at the cursor in the foreground app. The core transformation is
not "audio to text" as a commodity. It is human speech entering the text cursor and becoming
ordered language with less dependence on the keyboard.

## Primary User

A pt-BR developer who mixes English technical terms into speech and wants to move faster
across writing, coding, and AI prompting contexts. A future fork may become a medical
scribe for clinical notes, so the identity must support a more clinical variant without
becoming generic healthtech.

## Jobs To Be Done

- When typing interrupts thought, speak naturally so polished text appears at the cursor.
- When thinking in pt-BR but prompting AI tools in English, speak in pt-BR and receive a
  useful English prompt.
- When configuring the app, grant the required macOS permissions and API key once, then
  return to work.

## Brand Position

Intra Flow has the same DNA as Intrador but stands as an independent product. It inherits
ideas of internal flow, bioelectric impulse, membrane behavior, and fingerprint-like
fluidity from the Intrador transformation narrative without copying the Intrador mark.

## Strategic Identity Thesis

The canonical mark is kinetic. The `I` in Intra is a living text cursor: it waits for
keyboard input, receives human voice instead, and organizes that flow into language.

Flow without orientation disperses. Flow with order creates. Intra Flow is the internal
axis that turns natural speech into structured text.

## Anti-References

- Generic microphone, waveform, speech bubble, and AI sparkle marks.
- A literal medical logo, clinical-blue SaaS palette, or generic healthtech calm.
- A developer-only terminal identity that cannot extend to a medical scribe fork.
- Decorative motion that does not express state, listening, ordering, or creation.
